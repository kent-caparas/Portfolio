> I'm starting fresh, a reason to build more, and finally put my thoughts somewhere they can breathe

## Introduction
For those who doesn't know me, allow me to introduce myself - I'm Kent Hudson Caparas, I'm someone who could be unhinged, and at the same time be 
exceptionally shy. To be fair, I don't even know why. That's why I'm starting a fresh, who knows maybe writing some blog posts will eventually
cure me of this issue. 

I love building things, use cases excite me. The euphoric sensation of having a use case pop up in your head anytime makes me
want to stay holed into my small designated office. I follow tips, touching grass helps, but it doesn't help me stay away from my desk, because that's the
time when a use case pops in! :*cries*: 

I have a lovely wife, and I'm about to be a father soon! I can't tell the gender, but I'm pretty sure I'm going to be a great dad. Although my wife is
scared that I'll be teaching programming languages instead of ABC's. I mean who can resist? Either way, I'll let my kid choose what they want. 
Speaking of building things for people I love: *that same drive is what I do for work.*

## What I do professionally
*Picture this:* a financial analyst manually copying data between three systems, every single day, for years. That's the kind of workflow I get to blow up.
I'm an AI Forward Developer at Trelent Inc. We build private AI platforms, the kind that run in your isolated environment, 
not someone else's cloud, and we wire AI into the workflows that are quietly draining your team's hours. 
Financial services, legal, cybersecurity. Different clients, *same problem:* **valuable people doing repetitive things.**

My role sits at the intersection of that AI layer and the interface people actually use. I build the Next.js surfaces, 
design the integrations, and make sure the automation feels like it belongs, not like it was plastered on.
